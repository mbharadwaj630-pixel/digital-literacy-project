# Social Media Etiquette Checklist
### Student Digital Ambassador Initiative | VIT Bhopal University

As a Student Digital Ambassador, I follow these guidelines to maintain a professional digital footprint and ensure online safety for myself and my peers.

---

| **Do's (Professional & Academic Success)** | **Don'ts (Security & Reputation Risks)** |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Professional Branding:** Use a high-quality headshot for LinkedIn and GitHub profiles. | **Data Privacy:** Never post sensitive university documents, exam papers, or login OTPs. |
| **Communication Tone:** Proofread every post and comment for grammar and a professional tone. | **Informal Language:** Avoid using "text-speak," slang, or excessive emojis when messaging faculty. |
| **Showcase Work:** Share personal technical projects, such as my Python-based Tic-Tac-Toe game. | **Online Conflict:** Don't engage in public arguments or "vent" about classes and professors. |
| **Proper Attribution:** Always tag and credit others when sharing their technical work or ideas. | **Inappropriate Content:** Don't post photos or videos from campus that could be seen as unprofessional. |
| **Account Security:** Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on all social and financial accounts. | **Phishing Links:** Never click or share unverified "giveaway" links or suspicious WhatsApp groups. |

---

### **Personal Commitment**
As a B.Tech student specializing in **AI & ML**, I understand that my digital footprint is a long-term asset. I commit to using these platforms to build a professional identity that reflects my technical skills and my responsibility as a member of the VIT Bhopal community.
