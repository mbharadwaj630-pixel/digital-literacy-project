# Stay Safe Online: 8-Tip Prevention Checklist
### Student Digital Ambassador Initiative | VIT Bhopal University
Follow these essential rules to protect your financial and digital identity from evolving cyber threats.

---

1. **PIN is Only for Sending:** Remember that you **never** need to enter your UPI PIN or scan a QR code to *receive* money.

2. **Verify the Recipient:** Always double-check the recipient's name displayed directly on your banking app screen before hitting 'Pay'.

3. **Use Official Apps Only:** Never download "remote access" or "screen-sharing" apps (like AnyDesk) on the instructions of a stranger claiming to be "customer support".

4. **Check Official Bank SMS:** Confirm that funds have actually been credited by checking your bank's official SMS or app balance, rather than trusting a sender's screenshot.

5. **Decline 'Collect' Requests:** Be extremely wary of unexpected notifications asking you to "Approve" or "Authorize" a payment request.

6. **Secure Your Device:** Use biometric locks and enable **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)** for all financial, academic, and social media accounts.

7. **Limit Daily Transactions:** Set sensible daily transaction limits on your UPI apps to minimize potential losses in the event of a security breach.

8. **Report to 1930:** If fraud occurs, immediately call the **National Cybercrime Helpline at 1930** or visit **cybercrime.gov.in** within the "Golden Hour" to freeze the transaction.
